THE MESSAGE

He came to earth for you and I,
He appeared to us as a human kind.
“Who is this, that even the wind obeys him?”,
Who is this, that all authority in Heaven above and on earth below belongs to him?
Who is this that all creation bows at the mention of his name?
His name is Jesus. He who was without sin but became sin for you and I.
He died on the cross and rose again so we could be saved.
Saved from what? From eternal damnation, from sin and all it craved.
This is the message, this is the Gospel- the message of truth that the world needs to hear.
If only you would believe and confess that Jesus is Lord,
That he died and rose again for your salvation, that he is your redeemer and saviour,
Then he will be yours today. He will embrace you as one of his own,
Just accept him today, accept this message and live as one for whose sins have been atoned.
Jesus loves you, he is waiting for you with opened arms. Do not resist him when you see or hear THE MESSAGE.

TOMORROW MAY BE TOO LATE.
